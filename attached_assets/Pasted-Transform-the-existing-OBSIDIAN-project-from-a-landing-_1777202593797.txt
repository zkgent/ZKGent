Transform the existing OBSIDIAN project from a landing page plus                
access/onboarding app into a real product-oriented web application.             
                                                                                
Critical instructions                                                           
                                                                                
- Do not remove the existing landing page                                       
- keep / as the marketing homepage                                              
- preserve the OBSIDIAN visual identity and premium design language             
- reuse the existing codebase, routes, components, and styles where practical   
- remove all “waitlist”, “request early access”, “application received”, and    
MVP-style onboarding language                                                   
- this should no longer feel like an early-access startup preview               
- it should feel like a real product you can enter and use immediately          
                                                                                
Product direction                                                               
                                                                                
OBSIDIAN is a confidential payments platform for organizations operating on     
Solana.                                                                         
                                                                                
At this stage, do not pretend the full zero-knowledge settlement engine is live 
if it is not.                                                                   
But the product experience itself must now feel real, useful, and operational.  
                                                                                
This means:                                                                     
- no waitlist language                                                          
- no access-request flow                                                        
- no “apply for early access”                                                   
- no fake investor-demo onboarding                                              
- no token/trading/exchange UI                                                  
                                                                                
Instead, the app should become a real operator-facing confidential finance      
console.                                                                        
                                                                                
The user should be able to enter the product directly from the landing page and 
understand what the platform does, what workflows exist, and how it would be    
used in practice.
